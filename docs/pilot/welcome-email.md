Thank you for joining the ClassBridge pilot program! We're excited to have you help us shape the future of how parents stay connected to their children's education.

## What is ClassBridge?

ClassBridge is an education platform that gives you a single place to:

- **See your child's assignments** and due dates across all their courses
- **Message teachers** directly, right from your phone
- **Track tasks and progress** so nothing falls through the cracks
- **Stay on top of what's happening** with a daily dashboard and calendar view

## Getting Started

We've made it easy to get started. You can access ClassBridge two ways:

### On Your Computer (Web App)
Visit **https://www.classbridge.ca** and log in with the account credentials we've provided.

### On Your Phone (Mobile App)
We have a mobile app available through Expo Go — a free app that lets you run ClassBridge on your iPhone or Android. See the attached **Quick-Start Guide** for step-by-step setup instructions.
